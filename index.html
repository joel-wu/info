Research

My previous experience includes vision and remote sensing projects, where I contributed to:

  1. Interpretation of subpixel multi-spectral satellite maps, crucial for land resource management, geological disaster monitoring, and urban planning.

  2. Generation of hyperspectral images, providing valuable and reliable training data for data-driven models.
